What is Ambrosia?

Ambrosia is an wearable TUI that generates soothing vibrations to users who need calm and relaxation away from the screens. With its discrete calming effect and nature inspired designs, it provides a balance between necessary relaxation and intense focus

Problem Statement

For many students and working professionals, the tools of modern work, socialization, and leisure have been coalesced into the same set of screens. This results in less time spent in the calming and inspiring effects of nature. More importantly, the tools that keep us accountable and calm us down are on the same interface as our distractions. The poximity of these distractions affects productivity by making it more difficult to achieve a flow state of deep creative work. The resulting stress and anxiety creates a mental toll on our wellbeing as is evident from negative effects of being on screens for too long. We wanted to help users increase productivity and alleviate stress through our wearable TUI
